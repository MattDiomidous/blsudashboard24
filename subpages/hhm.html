"HHM"

About

Hispanic Heritage Month celebrates the history, culture, and contributions of Hispanics around the world. HHM starts on September 15 as it is the independence day of five Latin countries, Costa Rica, El Salvador, Guatemala, Honduras, and Nicaragua. Within the rest of the 30 days, many other Latin countries celebrate independence from Spain as well, such as Mexico on September 16 and Chile on September 18! Dia de la Raza or “Race Day” is celebrated on October 12 to recognize the cultures that make up Hispanics around the world, their indigenous roots, and European History.
